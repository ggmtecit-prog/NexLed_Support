Warranty & Guarantee

Below is Frequently Asked Questions, along with clear and concise answers. It provides quick access to essential information, helping clarify common doubts and offering straightforward guidance on the most important topics.

  • •

    Coverage

    The warranty applies to defects in materials and workmanship when the product is used under normal operating conditions. It does not cover damages that stem from misuse or environmental factors.

  • •

    Exclusions

    This warranty does not cover damage resulting from misuse, improper installation, accidents, unauthorized modifications, or exposure to conditions outside the recommended operating envelope. Products altered or repaired without Nexled authorization are generally not covered for warranty review.

  • •

    Request Warranty Service

    To request warranty service, gather the product model, serial number, and proof of purchase, then contact the support team. Support will send detailed instructions on how to return the product or obtain a direct service if applicable.

  • •

    Required Documentation

    You must provide proof of purchase, a clear description of the issue, a copy of the date, and the product serial number. Keep this information handy prior to contacting our warranty service department.

  • •

    Product Lifespan & Performance Expectations

    Nexled products are designed to deliver lasting, safe performance when used under recommended conditions. Any reduction in output or expected performance that is not associated to a direct performance lapse or fault is not considered a notable product performance disturbance.
